Welcome to the second chapter of Module 3! In this chapter, we'll explore NVIDIA Isaac Sim and how it's used to create photorealistic simulations and generate synthetic data for training AI perception models.

By the end of this chapter, you'll be able to:
- Explain the concept of photorealistic simulation
- Understand how synthetic data is generated for AI models
- Describe how to train perception systems safely in simulation
- Identify the benefits of simulation-based training

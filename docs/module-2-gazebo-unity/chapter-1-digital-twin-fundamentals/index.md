# Chapter 1: Digital Twin Fundamentals

Welcome to the first chapter of Module 2! In this chapter, we'll explore the fundamental concepts of Digital Twins and why they are essential for robotics development.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you'll be able to:
- Define what a Digital Twin is and explain its importance in robotics
- Understand the difference between simulation and real-world robotics
- Describe the roles of Gazebo and Unity in creating comprehensive simulation environments
- Explain how Digital Twins accelerate robot development using real-world analogies
